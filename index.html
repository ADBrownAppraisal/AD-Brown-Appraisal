Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need a certified home appraisal?
An independent appraisal ensures that financial lenders, courts, or property buyers receive an objective valuation to mitigate property investment risks.
How long does a standard property appraisal take?
While the on-site walkthrough inspection takes roughly 30–60 minutes, the complete verified report takes 3–5 business days to meticulously finalize.

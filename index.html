Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common inquiries about real estate valuations.

How long does a home appraisal report take?

Typically, our property assessment takes around 24-48 hours post-inspection to deliver a thoroughly researched, validated formal document.

What do appraisers look for during an on-site property inspection?

We analyze property size, architectural layout, health/safety conditions, interior upgrades, building materials, and physical components matching local market indicators.

Why are appraisals needed for refinancing?

Lenders require an independent equity assessment to verify that the home's market value adequately collateralizes the requested mortgage product loan balance amount.

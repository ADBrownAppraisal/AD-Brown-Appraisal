Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know About the Appraisal Process

What is a home appraisal?
A home appraisal is an unbiased professional opinion of a home's value based on similar, recent property sales in the immediate neighborhood, physical conditions, and structural evaluations.
Why do I need a certified appraisal report?
Certified reports ensure that lenders, courts, or tax boards receive accurate and legal valuation structures for home purchases, estate setups, refinancing, or divorce proceedings.
How long does an appraisal process take?
An on-site property inspection typically ranges from 30 minutes to a few hours, while the comprehensive final certified documentation is finalized within a few business days.
